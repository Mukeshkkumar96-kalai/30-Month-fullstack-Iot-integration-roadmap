## Today topic 
- To make the basic series led circuit

## Key concept I learned
- Basics of breadboard
- series connection
- Ohm's law basic

## Core idea
- To connect the LED in a series connection

## Problem Faced
- I used the (MB-102 Bread board – 830) breadboard so the connection is too different from the gendral breadboard.
- Problem: To connect the power source in the breadboard that not gave the power supply.(For the normal breadboard for the power source we connect to starting of the circuit and ending of the circuit)

## Solution
- For the power supply I use the top or bottom of the breadboard for the connection.
- For the positive to connect in the red line or the "+" symbol for the navigation.
- For the negative to connect in the red line or the "-" symbol for the navigation.
